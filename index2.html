Who I Am
Operations runs in my blood

I grew up in a family where business was daily life. My father ran a utensil and home appliances shop in Nagpur — and from an early age I was managing stock, suppliers, customers and daily operations. This is not something I studied from the outside. I lived it from childhood.

Over four years at I Fly Aviation, I managed operations for 300+ training sessions, drove 25+ process improvements across three departments and maintained 98% record accuracy across 500+ files — building systems from scratch.

At RIVIGO — India’s pioneering relay trucking company — I managed end-to-end fleet operations for Amul. I redesigned dispatch scheduling and reduced fleet idle time by 18% with data-driven routing.

Then I made the biggest bet of my life. I self-funded over €25,000 to move to France — completely alone. No scholarship. No safety net. I earned a place at ESSCA School of Management, triple-accredited AACSB, AMBA and EQUIS — and I received their scholarship.
